Omelette with Cheese

Description

This Omelette with Cheese is a fluffy, airy omelette that melts in your mouth. Perfect for breakfast or brunch, this dish combines light, whipped eggs with your favorite cheese for a delightful and elegant meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ¼ cup grated cheese (cheddar, Gruyère, or your favorite)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh herbs (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Separate the egg yolks and whites into two bowls.
  2. Whisk the egg yolks with the milk until well combined, then season with salt and pepper.
  3. Using an electric mixer, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  4. Gently fold the beaten egg whites into the yolk mixture, being careful not to deflate the egg whites.
  5. Heat the butter in a non-stick frying pan over medium-low heat until melted and slightly bubbling.
  6. Pour the egg mixture into the pan, spreading it out evenly. Cook for 3-4 minutes, or until the bottom is set and lightly golden.
  7. Sprinkle the grated cheese over one half of the omelette, then carefully fold the other half over the cheese.
  8. Cover the pan with a lid and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the cheese is melted and the omelette is cooked through.
  9. Slide the omelette onto a plate, garnish with chopped fresh herbs if desired, and serve immediately.
